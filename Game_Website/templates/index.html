Competition Crawl: E-Sports vs. Sports

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to compare and contrast the viewership numbers of E-sports and American football. The website will present data and statistics on the number of viewers for popular e-sports competitions, such as the League of Legends World Championship, and American football events. Additionally, the website will provide analysis and commentary on the trends and growth of both industries. The comparison of these viewership numbers aims to shed light on the increasing popularity and mainstream acceptance of e-sports and its potential to challenge traditional sports as a major form of entertainment and competition.
